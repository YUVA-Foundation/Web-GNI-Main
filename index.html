About Institution


Guru Nanak Institutions (GNI), Nagpur is premier educational group dedicated to provide quality education in the disciplines of Engineering & Management. The institutes are approved by All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), New Delhi and Directorate of Technical Education (DTE) Maharashtra, NAAC Accredited and are affiliated to Rashtrasant Tukdoji Maharaj Nagpur University (RTMNU), Nagpur.

The Campus is spread across a land of 12.5 acres on Katol Road, at a distance of 16 km from main city. The site is picturesque, serene and offers clean and green environment.
